Week 9 - Progress Update

What We Achieved This Week

This week on our Satellite Orbit and Event Predictor (SOEP) project we've been focusing on some exciting new improvements and updates. Here's what we've accomplished:

  • Bigger Database: Our database now contains more satellites and gets updated regulary to always be able to display the satellites and their future orbits correctly.
  • Database Refactor: Our database has undergone significant refactoring, resulting in faster build times and better performance.
  • User Location: Added support for getting user location which will be used in future updates, stay tuned!
  • New logo: Our new logo is finalized!
