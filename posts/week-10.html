Week 10 - Progress Update

What We Achieved This Week

This week, as part of our Satellite Orbit and Event Predictor (SOEP) project, we focused on several key improvements to both the backend and frontend. Here's a summary of what we accomplished:

  • Backend Enhancements: Improved code quality through refactoring, fixing warnings, and optimizing functionality.
  • UI Updates: Designed the predictions tab, updated the header design, and reworked the viewport gizmo for a cleaner look.
  • New Features: Added a connecting line from the Earth to satellites when clicked and fixed a bug where orbit lines didn't start correctly from the satellite's position.
  • Popup Improvements: Redesigned and moved the popup to the right side, added a window extension feature, and introduced hover text for all icons.
  • Animated Logo: Updated the animated logo to rely solely on CSS, eliminating the need for JavaScript.
